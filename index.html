Wilderness Camping Close to the Coast

Broad Creek Campground offers a quiet retreat with modern comforts, easy access to the coast, and space to unwind the way you like it.

About Broad Creek Campground

Broad Creek Campground is designed to give campers a comfortable stay without losing that “out in the woods” feeling. Conveniently located close to the coast, we offer over 50 camp sites and access to on-site amenities so you can enjoy your time without overcomplicating it.

Amenities

RV & Tent Sites

Spacious sites with power and water hookups available for RVs.

Bath House

Clean restrooms and hot showers to keep everyone comfortable during their stay.

Pool

Seasonal pool for cooling off. Unguarded — swim at your own risk.

Fishing & Creek Access.

Access to Broad Creek for licensed recreational fishing and water access in designated areas, subject to campground rules and waivers.

Trails

On-site trails for walking and exploring the property.

Arcade & Camp Store

Arcade for the kids (and adults) and a camp store for essentials you forgot at home.

Dock & Boat Ramp

Creekside dock and ramp access for approved watercraft, subject to safety rules and signed waivers.

Rules & Policies

test

Check-In / Check-Out

  • Check-in: 12:00 PM – 5:00 PM.
  • Check-out: before 11:00 AM.
  • Late arrivals must make prior arrangements with campground management.

Site Use

  • One camping unit per site unless otherwise approved.
  • Keep all vehicles and equipment on the designated pad or driveway.
  • Do not cut, damage, or nail into trees or campground structures.
  • Keep your site clean and bag all trash for proper disposal.

Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours are typically from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM.
  • No loud music, generators, or disruptive behavior during quiet hours.
  • Be respectful of neighboring campers at all times.

Pool & Water Safety

  • The pool is unguarded. Swim at your own risk.
  • Children must be supervised by an adult at all times in the pool area.
  • No diving, glass containers, or rough play in the pool area.
  • No swimming in the creek. Entry into the creek by watercraft (e.g., kayak) requires a signed liability waiver.

Fishing & Hunting

  • Recreational fishing is permitted only with a valid license and in designated areas.
  • Follow all state and local regulations regarding seasons and limits.
  • Hunting on the property is subject to written permission, town rules, and any lease or permit conditions in effect.

Pets

  • Pets must be kept on a leash and under control at all times.
  • Clean up after your pet immediately.
  • Excessive barking or aggressive behavior may result in removal from the campground.

Fires & Firewood

  • Fires are allowed only in designated fire rings or fire pits.
  • Never leave a fire unattended; fully extinguish before leaving or going to sleep.
  • Follow any posted burn bans or restrictions.

Alcohol, Smoking & Substances

  • Alcohol use must be responsible and confined to your site.
  • Follow all local and state laws for alcohol and tobacco use.
  • No illegal drugs anywhere on the property.

Liability & Enforcement

  • Guests camp at their own risk; management is not responsible for lost or damaged property.
  • Violation of rules may result in removal from the campground without refund.
  • Additional policies may be provided in writing at check-in.
